Strategy 1: Uncover the Real Objection

Often, "I need to think about it" is a polite way of saying something else. Your job is to gently probe and find out what the real concern is. Is it price? Is it timing? Do they need to speak with a partner?

"I completely understand. Usually when I hear that, it means there's a specific concern about either the price or the fit. Could you share what's on your mind?"
