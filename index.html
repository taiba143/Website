Hi, I'm Taiba Mahmood – Sales Coach for Coaches.

But before I helped hundreds close confidently... I was once a girl staring out of her cubicle window, wondering when she’d finally feel free.

My Journey

Back in 2018, I was working as a software engineer at TCS. Everything looked "settled" on the outside, but inside I felt stuck and suffocated. I kept wondering, "When will I finally get to do something of my own?"

I applied to EDHEC Business School in France for a master's in marketing – and I got in. It felt like the dream was finally happening. Until... my loan was rejected. My scholarship was canceled. My family couldn't afford the fees. I was devastated. I had no direction.

Then a mentor gave me a nudge: "Why not teach public speaking? You're amazing at it." He was right. I had reached the District Finals of Toastmasters International and spoken on global stages. So I began coaching others in public speaking.

The Pivot

I discovered personal branding. I built a strong brand, helped others build theirs, and watched my students attract business opportunities they never imagined. But I wanted more. I dove into business skills: marketing, ads, psychology, sales. And soon, I noticed something important: Coaches were amazing at their craft, but struggled to SELL it.

So I trained with top global sales mentors, sold over $10,000 worth of high-ticket programs, and started coaching others in sales. My clients began closing INR 50K to 1L+ deals confidently. That’s when I knew—this is my calling.

Biggest Lesson?

You don’t have to have the whole path figured out. Just take the first step. The rest unfolds as you move.
